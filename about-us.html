About Us

What we do

HR Recruitment Solutions provide a supportive process for clients and candidates alike, matching skills, aspirations and styles to allow both the employee and employer to thrive.

Why

Building on our founders’ experience of working with HR professionals from CHROs of blue chip multinationals to HR administrators of localised SMEs, HR Recruitment Solutions was set up to be more than just another recruitment company.

How

By adding value beyond the norm and positioning themselves as a key strategic recruitment partner, HR Recruitment Solutions are uniquely positioned to attract talent beyond the results from a recruitment agency or advertisement.

Founder - Leon Morley

Its founder, Leon Morley approached his career in a different way to the ‘average’ recruiter. Leon progressed in his recruitment career by focusing on pursuing long-term relationships as a trusted partner to businesses and HR professionals.
